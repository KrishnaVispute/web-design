Jawaharlal nehru

Great indian politician and India's first and longest serving prime minister.

About the legend

1. Jawaharlal Nehru was born on 14th november 1889 in Allahabad in British India.

2. His father Motilal Nehru was prominent lawyer who blonged to kashmiri pandit community.

3. Jawaharlal Nehru was educated in england at harrow school And tinity college, cambridge.

4. He became barrrister, retuned to india,enrolled at Allahabad high court and gradually began to take interest in politics.

5. He was india's first and the longest serving prime minister.

6. He served as prime minister of india for 16 years.

7. He died on 27th May 1964.The cause of his death was heart attack.

8. He is referred to as most significant figure of indian independance movement.

9. In 1955, Nehru was awarded with the Bharat Ratna Award
